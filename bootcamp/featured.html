GOLD BOND BRANDING

PROJECT INTRO

GoldBond & Molson joined with us to create a new advertising campaign for their brand new product mashup. They needed a fresh set of ads, along with a promotional video to announce the mashup and new formula. There is no better way to kick off the summer then to work with our favourite beer brand!

PROJECT DESCRIPTION

We wanted to keep the look of the Molson Brand but bring in some elements of the GoldBond brand to compliment it. Everyone knows Molson so we wanted to keep some recognition throughout the designs. We decided to keep the ads light with some references to current events.

PROJECT OUTRO

Working with GoldBond X Molson was a blast, here at Smart Gorilla we strive for the best designs and top priority is to leave our clients satisfied and happy with their experiences with us. To plan your very own project with us, go on over to our contact page and drop us a line!
